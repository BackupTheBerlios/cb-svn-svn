cb-svn

code::blocks::subversion

cb-svn is a plugin providing access to the revision control sytem subversion for the code::blocks integrated development environment. TortoiseSVN will be auto-detected and used if it is present.
In addition, for those who are still bound to use cvs, the most often used cvs commands will be supported. Due to limitations imposed by cvs, however, much of the "better" functionality is not available to cvs users.
